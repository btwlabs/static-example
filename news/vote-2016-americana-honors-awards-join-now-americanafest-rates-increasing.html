Vote for the 2016 Americana Honors & Awards; Join Now; AmericanaFest Rates Increasing

// 5.13.16

You Must be a Member to Vote for the 2016 Americana Honors & Awards

Voting for the 2016 Americana Honors & Awards will begin on May 16, 2016. You will need to be an Americana Music Association member by May 18, 2016 to participate in this process. Voting will end on May 26, 2016.

Click here to see a full list of those nominated for the 2016 Americana Honors & Awards.

Did you miss our nominee announcement? Watch it here! Features performances by Margo Price, Sierra Hull & Ethan Jodziewicz, The Milk Carton Kids, and Lucinda Williams.

Join the Americana Music Association Now

Want to vote for the Honors & Awards and receive the member registration rate, but you’re not a member? Click here to join now.

We offer two membership levels: Artist/Musician/Songwriter and Industry. With your membership you are eligible to:

-Attend AmericanaFest at a discounted rate
-Meet and connect with other members of the Americana community
-Enjoy exclusive benefits and discounts
-Attend events and network with others who share your passion
-Vote and participate in the Americana nominating and voting processes

 

AmericanaFest Registration Rates Increasing

Only registrants have the ability to purchase (up to two) tickets to the Honors & Awards show.

May 15, 2016 is the last day to take advantage of our early bird registration rates. Right now, members can register for $225, while non-members can register for $325. On May 16, 2016, the rates will increase to $275 and $375 respectively. AmericanaFest will be held in Nashville, TN September 20-25, 2016.

Purchase of a registration gives you access to the following:
-ALL daytime events at the conference center
-ALL evening music festival showcases
-ALL offsite events (unless noted)
-ONE goody bag
-ONE conference program guide
*You also have the option to purchase up to TWO Honors & Awards show tickets and wristbands for guests while supplies last.

Click here to register now!
