Caitlin Canty w/ special guests Oshima Brothers Presented by the Americana Music Association®

1.4.2019 to 1.19.2019

Join Us in Celebrating the Americana Tour Blueprint Alongside Caitlin Canty with special guests Oshima Brothers

To commemorate the launch of this new member benefit, we're presenting an inaugural 2019 tour featuring Caitlin Canty with special guests Oshima Brothers.

We're thrilled to have these two acts be the first to use this new resource to book their run of shows set to embark on January 4. (Please see below for a complete list of dates.)

Tickets for these shows are on sale now and available for purchase at www.caitlincanty.com.

Members of the Americana Music Association will receive a special discount code to purchase tickets via the email address associated with their membership account on November 1. If you'd like to join to take advantage of this exclusive discount, become a member here by 2pm CDT on Wednesday, Oct. 31.  

Caitlin Canty with special guests Oshima Brothers on Tour (2019)
Presented by the Americana Music Association:

January 4: New York City, NY - Rockwood Music Hall 

January 5: Cooperstown, NY - Cooperstown Concert Series at The Otesaga Resort Hotel*

January 6: Saratoga Springs, NY - Caffe Lena

January 8: Boston, MA - City Winery**

January 10: Philadelphia, PA - The Locks at Sona

January 11: Vienna, VA - Jammin' Java

January 12: Charlottesville, VA - Southern Cafe

January 13: Roanoke, VA - The Spot on Kirk

January 15: Charlotte, NC - The Evening Muse 

January 16: Asheville, NC - Isis Music Hall

January 17: Decatur, GA - Eddie's Attic 

January 18: Chattanooga, TN - Songbirds North Stage 

January 19: Nashville, TN - Analog at The Hutton Hotel

* = No member discount will be offered for this show
** = Tickets for this show go on sale November 1
